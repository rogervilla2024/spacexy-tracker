The X/Y Axis Innovation

Space XY's signature feature is its dual-axis multiplier display - the innovation that gives the game its name.

Rather than displaying a simple linear multiplier (like Aviator's curve), Space XY tracks the rocket's position on two coordinates:

  • Y-Axis: Vertical altitude reached

The combination of these coordinates produces the multiplier value.
