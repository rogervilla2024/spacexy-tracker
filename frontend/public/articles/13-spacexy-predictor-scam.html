Why Predictors Can't Work

1. Cryptographic RNG

Space XY uses cryptographically secure random number generators. These cannot be predicted - they're the same technology used in banking and military encryption.

2. Server-Side Generation

Outcomes are generated on BGaming's servers, not your device. No app or extension can access or predict server-side data.

3. Provably Fair Verification

Each round's outcome is determined before you play and can be cryptographically verified. If prediction was possible, the system would be broken.

4. Independent Rounds

Each round is statistically independent. Past results have zero influence on future outcomes. "Pattern recognition" is useless.
