Coordinate System Explained

Space XY displays X and Y coordinates as you travel:

  • X Coordinate: Horizontal position in space
  • Y Coordinate: Vertical position (altitude)
  • Higher coordinates = higher multiplier
  • Purely visual - doesn't affect odds
